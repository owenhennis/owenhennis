Hiking


I grew up going on hikes with my parents, and I loved being outdoors to climb on things, jump around, and be outside in the sun. As a child, the outdoors was my ultimate playground—a place to climb, jump, and discover. I took my role as a 'Junior Ranger' at National Parks seriously, developing an early commitment to environmental stewardship and active exploration. Nowadays, hiking is one of my favorite things to do with friends. It's a great way to go out and see what nature has to offer. Lately I've enjoyed visiting national parks, checking different ones off as I go. In the future I hope to continue to travel to new places and parks, and to continue to explore the outdoors with friends and family.
