About Me

I grew up in Arvada, Colorado, and at a young age, I learned how to find enjoyment in everything. I love being outside, whether it's playing sports, hiking, running, biking, or snowboarding. I have always had a passion for traveling, sightseeing, and exploring new places. I've been involved in sports my whole life, starting soccer at age four; it's been a huge part of my life, giving me lifelong friends and memories. I'm a very social guy—I like talking to people, and I'm great at developing communities, whether through sports, at work, with friends, or even with strangers.
