Soccer


Soccer has been the most influential part of my life. I started playing when I was four, starting at the Junior Rapids, and then playing for the Arvada Edge for the rest of my club career. I've had memorable laughs, goals, injuries, and heartbreaks, but I'm thankful for every single moment. It's given me lifelong friends, unforgettable memories, and above all it's shaped who I am as a person today.
