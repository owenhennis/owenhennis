Travel


Traveling is my favorite thing to do in the world. I love going to a new place and exploring it to the most of my capability. Not only is it the place, but it's also the people that you go with, and the memories that are made together. One feeling that I really enjoy is looking back at pictures that were taken and seeing the smiles and cool places. These travels have shaped my perspective, teaching me that every new location is an opportunity to learn about culture, traditions, and geography. I have been fortunate enough to go to some amazing places, and I can't wait to see where I will go in the future.
